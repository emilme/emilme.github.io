About

Policy program associate at New America's Open Technology Institute. I like to think about the Internet.

Before joining New America, I was a Fulbright Scholar in South Korea, researching the sharing economy and the Korean efforts to seed a startup ecosystem in Seoul. I'm an alumna of Yale and the Harpeth Hall School, and hail from Nashville, TN.

I've previously worked with the Berkman Center for Internet and Society's Chilling Effects project, and helped build SF Open Law for the Mayor's Office of Civic Innovation in San Francisco.
