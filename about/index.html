About

I like to think about the Internet, law, cities, and the environment. Currently based in Washington, D.C. as a policy program associate at New America's Open Technology Institute. Before joining New America, I was a Fulbright Scholar in South Korea, researching the sharing economy and the Korean efforts to seed a startup ecosystem in Seoul. I'm an alumna of Yale and the Harpeth Hall School, and hail from Nashville, TN.
